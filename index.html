About Me

I am an avid equestrian and am building an app to resolve problems for people who show. will then use the profits to rescue horses from slaughter. I started my career creating Websites and then fell into enterprise software sales. After something like 25 years doing that, I amassed enough funds to invest in a startup which is developing an app to do three things:
1. Take the stress out of equestrian competitions.
2. Become a profitable company by introducing a more ethical method of marketing.
3. Use those profits to save horses from slaughter.
